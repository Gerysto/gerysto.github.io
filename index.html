About this website

Welcome to my personal website! I created this space as a way to share some of my programming projects in an interactive way, so visitors can try them out directly rather than just reading about them. Right now, the site is still brand new, so the content is limited, but my goal is to keep expanding it over time as I build more projects.

Think of it as a growing collection of experiments, ideas, and coding challenges that I've enjoyed working on, and a way for me to showcase my progress along the way.
